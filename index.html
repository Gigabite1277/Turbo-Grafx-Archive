Welcome to the Turbo Grafx Archive The PC Engine is one of the best consoles of the 1980's that you've probabley never heard of. A raging success in Japan and Asia, with a cult following around Europe made this console the talk of 1980's gaming culture, that's if you could find one. Boasting graphics abilities above anything at that time the PC Engine/Turbo Grafx was a must for all who encountered one. So please take a look around the site, where you find information and videos about this amazing video games console.
